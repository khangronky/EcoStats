Our goals

At EcoStats, our mission is straightforward: to make it easy for everyone - from governments and scientists to regular folks - to look at how the temperature of the ocean and land has changed over years. We believe data is a super useful tool to understand things and get people to take action. So, we made a website where you can check out different facts and info about temperatures in different countries in a simple way. Our goal is to help all kinds of people, whether you just want a quick overview or a deep dive into the details. By sharing our info and ideas, we hope to get people interested, talking, and working together. Together, we can do things that make a real difference and keep our planet safe for the future.


You can go to the Data page and use the filter to view our available data, there are basic and advance functions for both regular people and researchers who want a deeper insight into the data

Who may use our website?

  • Skills & Expeience levels: Emma is adept at using the web for information gathering but avoids getting into the nitty-gritty of data analysis. She's proficient in general web navigation but stays away from technicalities that require expert understanding.
