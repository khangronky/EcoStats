Welcome to EcoStats!

We provide data of changes in ocean and land tempature over a 260-year period.
You can explore and compare data from different geographic locations and time periods, and learn how temperature trends affect different regions and populations.
Whether you are a student, a researcher, or just a curious individual, EcoStats can help you discover and understand the impact of climate change on our planet.

To start exploring, click on the Go to Data button below.
